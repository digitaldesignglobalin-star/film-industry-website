About Us

Sobuj Films & Television Studio (SFTS) is a reputed and established production house founded & Produced by Sobuj Sarkar, dedicated to creating quality content for films and television.

From this banner, various feature films are produced with creativity, professionalism, and strong storytelling. Under the able leadership of Honourable Sobuj Sarkar, Sobuj Films & Television Studio has earned a respected name and credibility in the entertainment industry.

In addition to feature films, OTT web series are also produced, keeping pace with modern digital platforms and evolving audience preferences.

SAFA – Sobuj Art & Film Academy

SAFA (Sobuj Art & Film Academy) is a professional film and media training institute operating under the banner of Sobuj Films & Television Studio. The academy is dedicated to nurturing talent and transforming passionate individuals into confident, industry-ready professionals.

At SAFA, students receive comprehensive training with great care, dedication, and personal attention. Our courses are designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical experience, ensuring complete preparation for real industry challenges.

Our Areas of Expertise

  • Acting
  • Modeling
  • Direction
  • Scriptwriting
  • Dancing
  • Cinematography
  • Editing

Training at SAFA includes indoor studio practice as well as outdoor, on-location experience, allowing students to understand real shooting environments. This hands-on approach helps students gain confidence, discipline, and professional work ethics.

Students are trained by reputed directors and highly experienced theatre artists, who share their real-life industry experience, techniques, and creative insights. Special focus is given to personality development, camera facing skills, body language, dialogue delivery, expressions, and performance refinement.

One of the strongest pillars of SAFA is its 100% placement support. After successful training, students are provided opportunities in television serials, OTT platforms, web series, feature films, advertisements, and music videos based on their skills and performance.

“Learn, Practice, and Perform” – At SAFA, our goal is not only to teach but to create a clear pathway into the entertainment industry, making SAFA a trusted and result-oriented film academy.
